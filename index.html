Synthetic Biology New Zealand

Welcome to SynbioNZ! We are the NZ node of Synbio Australasia – a space where researchers, students, policy makers, and anyone interested in synthetic biology can connect and learn about synthetic biology in New Zealand.

We aim to advocate for synthetic biology research safety and awareness, create a space for NZ-based synthetic biological research to grow, and enable NZ-based synthetic biology to successfully create positive social and environmental impact on a global scale. Read about our Mission and Values

Synthetic Biology in Queenstown

Join us as we showcase the latest advancements in synthetic biology research in NZ, engage in discussions on GMO legislation and bolstering iGEM support in the Southern Hemisphere. Our goal is to foster an interdisciplinary community where diverse groups can exchange ideas and collectively problem-solve. Featuring speakers from across New Zealand and Australia, we are holding a two-day synthetic biology satellite as part of this years QMB.

What is synthetic biology
